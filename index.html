How orders work

Simple steps, no online payment.

1
Find a 3D model you like.
Browse Printables or Thingiverse and copy the link, or think of something you want.
2
Tell me what you want.
Talk to me in school, or contact me through this page, and show me the link or idea.
3
I check the print and give a quotation.
I look at the size and printing time, then give you a clear price and how long it will take. If you buy multiple, you could get a discount :)
4
We meet in school and you pay cash.
When the print is ready I bring it to school. You check it, and pay in cash.

Prices are guide prices. Very large or tricky models may cost more, but I will always confirm the price before printing.
