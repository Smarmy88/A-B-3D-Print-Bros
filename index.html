Print on demand!

3D prints made to order. Toys, tools, gadgets, decorations and more. Great fun projects for students.

My name is Aaron. I help turn your ideas into real 3D prints. You can send me a link to any model from Printables or Thingiverse, or simply describe what you want. I will check if the model is printable and tell you what is possible.

How it works

Simple steps to get something printed.

1
Find a model you like.
Browse Printables or Thingiverse, or think of an idea.
2
Show me the link or describe the idea.
Talk to me in school or share the link.
3
I check if it can be printed.
Some models may require permission depending on licensing.
4
I print it and bring it to school.
Simple, safe, and student-run.

Contact

Ask about a model or send a link.

WhatsApp or Instagram will be added later. For now, just talk to me in school and show me what you want printed.
